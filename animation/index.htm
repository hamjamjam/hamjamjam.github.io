River Flows in You

A study in 3D motion. With this creation, I aimed to capture the delicate nature of making music on the piano. I hoped to show the varied movment of piano keys once pressed down and released. This study examines the rendering of different surfaces and attention to detail in piano key movement. While fairly lengthy for a video of its type, I have tried to keep the viewer entertained with different angles slowly dragged across the screen, showing the fusion of shadows, lighting and reflective surfaces captured alongside the playing of the piano.
