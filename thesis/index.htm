One Size Does Not Fit All

Tools used: Rhino, Grasshopper, Illustrator

Innovation in emergency housing with a focus on Nepal

Every year millions of people are displaced due to natural disasters and transitional shelters exist to provide semi-permanent housing. This thesis addresses the problem of people needing housing and of unsafe transitional housing by presenting a new design process and shelter design specifically for the victims of the 2015 earthquakes in Nepal. The shelter design harnesses the structural strength of the geodesic dome, the simplicity of reciprocal joinery and strong yet lightweight nature of bamboo.
