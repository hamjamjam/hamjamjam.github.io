Antarctic Penetrator

Tools used: Rhino, Archsim, Waterjet, Electronics hardware

Data collection in extreme environments

Objectives were to collect data that would allow scientists at JPL to study the affects of climate change and to investiage penetrators as a method of data collection. Below is the system derived for powering the entire penetrator system. The system as a whole was designed to be dropped from an aircraft into the Ross Ice Shelf where it was to remain while collecting weather data and trasmitting that data back to a ground station.
